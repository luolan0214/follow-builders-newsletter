Levie 论证为什么 context 永远重要：不管模型多智能，都不能替代 context 的需求。任何足够通用的 AI 都需要你引导它——律师、工程师、金融分析师、医疗专业人员用的是同一个模型，但方向完全不同。

指令、领域上下文和专有数据永远需要进入 context window。这就是为什么 AI 自动化不是免费的，也是为什么谁在从 AI 获得收益、谁没有的差距如此之大。你必须投入真正的工作，才能在另一端获得真正的价值。216 赞。

Levie：不管模型多智能 context 永远需要。指令+领域上下文+专有数据进 context window。投入真正工作才能获得价值。216 赞。

English

Levie's thesis on why context will always matter: there's no amount of intelligence that replaces the need for context. For any sufficiently general-purpose AI, you will always have to guide it—lawyers, engineers, financial analysts, healthcare professionals all use the same model but need completely different directions.

Instructions, domain context, and proprietary data will always need to get into the context window. This is why AI automation doesn't come for free, and why there's a wide spectrum of who's getting gains and who's not. You have to put in real work, and you get real value on the other end. 216 likes.

Instructions, domain context, and proprietary data will always need to get into the context window for the model to be useful. You have to put in real work, and you get real value on the other end.
