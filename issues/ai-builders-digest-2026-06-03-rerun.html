English

Levie predicts that as token budgets take on a larger part of operating expenses, model routing is the inevitable conclusion. By understanding different work patterns in your domain and having strong evals, you'll be able to cost/performance optimize effectively.

Rauchg 把 AI 时代的教育框定为：从语言开始——语言表面就是你的路线图。就像 SDK 有一组函数定义一样，人类语言是通往世界的新 API。

English

Rauchg frames education in the age of AI: start with the language—the linguistic surface is your roadmap. Much like an SDK has function definitions, human language is the new API to the world.

His deeper point: there's never been an expert in any field without mastery over its language. The distinction is that English alone could not produce tangible things before. You had to translate it into machine instruction through learning or delegation. You can now go direct. 765 likes.
