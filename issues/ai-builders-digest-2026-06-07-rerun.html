Token Economics & Model Routing / Token 经济与模型路由

Levie 的长文和 RealMadhuGuru 的三阶段框架放在一起，把企业 token 策略讲得很清楚。

AL
Aaron Levie
CEO @ Box
@levie
中文

Levie 写了一篇长文说 token 成本正在成为他接触的任何企业最热的话题之一。这对 AI 整体来说非常利好，因为意味着这些系统正以前所未有的规模被使用。

这催生了应用 AI 层的另一种差异化：model routing。当 token 占了任何一个工作流成本的很大一部分时，公司就会想确保钱花在最有效的 token 使用上。前沿智能在高端任务上（编码、法律、金融分析、医疗）始终重要，但你可以把个别任务剥离给更便宜的模型。

拥有最好的 evals、最强的路由能力、以及商业模式与客户财务目标一致的公司，会处于很好的位置。404 赞。

Levie：token 成本是企业最热话题。AI 被以前未想到的规模使用。Model routing 成为差异化。最好的 evals 和路由能力的公司赢。404 赞。

English

Levie writes that token costs are becoming one of the hottest topics for any enterprise. This is very bullish for AI in general because it means systems are being used at a scale that wasn't contemplated before.

This gives way to another form of differentiation for the applied AI layer: model routing. As tokens take on a significant amount of cost, companies will want to ensure dollars go to the most efficient use of tokens. Frontier intelligence will always be relevant for high-end tasks like coding, legal, financial analysis, healthcare. But you can peel off individual tasks to lower-cost models.

The companies with the best evals, the best ability to route workloads, and business models aligned to customers' financial goals will be in a great position. 404 likes.

企业 model routing 三阶段：默认用最火模型→过度优化找最便宜的→精细化路由拆 sub-agent。企业滞后 6-9 月。

English

RealMadhuGuru adds the three-phase enterprise progression for model routing. Phase 1 (2024): default to the 'it' model, everyone used GPT regardless of task. Phase 2 (early 2025): over-optimize, teams looked for cheapest models but without sophisticated evals, ended up burning cycles and shipping slower. Phase 3: nuanced routing—AI-native startups break products into sub-agents and route each task to the right model (hardest reasoning to Claude, simplest to Gemini Flash-Lite or open-weight).

Enterprises followed the AI-native builders 6-9 months later.

Swyx 提出了一个关于为什么研究论文影响力在消亡的理论。

S
Swyx
Writer / Builder
@swyx
中文

Swyx 提出了一个理论：研究论文的 alpha 之所以在消亡，是因为研究者发现与其跟市场部争，不如直接出门拿超过 1 亿美元做创业——用他们合法保护的隐性知识。

Swyx：研究 alpha 死了因为研究者直接出门创业拿 $100M+。加州非竞业禁令比 GitHub+arXiv+HuggingFace 对知识传播影响更大。134 赞。

English

Swyx proposes a theory: research paper alpha died when researchers realized that instead of fighting with marketing departments, they could simply walk out the door and get >$100M for their legally protected tacit knowledge.

His claim: California non-noncompetes have a bigger impact on knowledge spreading than GitHub, arXiv, and HuggingFace combined. This motivates him to set up AI Engineer as a product-centric industry conference to complement paper-centric research conferences. 134 likes.
